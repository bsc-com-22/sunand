Find an item, negotiate with the seller, and initiate a transaction. Pay into Sogoloh's escrow account using mobile money or bank transfer. Only approve delivery after inspecting the item. Never send money outside the platform.

If the item isn't delivered within the agreed timeframe, report it immediately. Your funds remain in escrow and will be refunded to you after investigation. The seller's account may be suspended.

You have 24-48 hours (depending on the item category) to inspect and approve or reject the item after delivery. This gives you time to test electronics, check clothing fit, or verify product authenticity.

Funds are released to your Sogoloh wallet immediately after the buyer approves delivery. You can then withdraw to your mobile money account (Airtel Money, Mpamba) or bank account within 24 hours.

Sign up, verify your identity by uploading your National ID, and click "Post Listing". Add clear photos, detailed description, price, and condition. Verified sellers get more visibility.

Sogoloh charges a small service fee on successful transactions. The fee structure is transparent and shown before you confirm a sale. There are no hidden charges or listing fees.
