# Branding Guidelines: Sun & Soil Foundation

This document outlines the visual identity and branding standards for the Sun & Soil Foundation website.

## 1. Brand Essence

**Sun & Soil Foundation** is a Malawian non-profit organization dedicated to transforming food systems through clean energy, climate-smart agriculture, and capacity development.

### Vision

A Malawi where every community is food secure, climate-resilient, economically empowered, and powered by clean energy.

### Mission

To empower vulnerable households through solar-powered agriculture, climate-smart farming, and inclusive livelihood programmes.

## 3. Typography

The foundation uses a modern, high-legibility typographic system to ensure accessibility and a professional aesthetic.

### Display Font: **Plus Jakarta Sans**

* **Purpose:** Headings, Hero titles, and prominent interface elements.
* **Characteristics:** Modern geometric sans-serif with a friendly, open feel.
* **Weights:** 600 (Semi-bold) for subheads, 700 (Bold) for H1/H2 titles.

### Body Font: **Inter**

* **Purpose:** Body text, navigation, and functional UI elements.
* **Characteristics:** Exceptional legibility at small sizes, clean and neutral.
* **Weights:** 400 (Regular) for bulk text, 500 (Medium) for navigation.

## 4. Visual Language

### Shapes & Components

* **Rounded Corners:** A generous radius of `0.75rem` (`12px`) is used for cards and images to create a soft, welcoming feel.
* **Buttons:** Action buttons are fully rounded (pill-shaped) to distinguish them from content cards.
* **Elevation:** Subtle shadows (`shadow-soft`) used to separate content layers without adding visual clutter.

### Imagery Guidelines

* **Human-Centric:** Focus on the people of Malawi—farmers, women, and youth—engaged with technology and the land.
* **Brightness:** Images should be brightly lit, reflecting the "Sun" influence.
* **Authenticity:** Avoid generic stock photography; prefer real project photos from the field.
