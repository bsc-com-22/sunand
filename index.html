The Context

Why Our Work Matters

Malawi's agricultural sector faces increasing pressure from climate change, erratic rainfall, limited irrigation infrastructure, and widespread rural poverty. Millions of smallholder farmers remain dependent on rain-fed agriculture, making food security and household incomes vulnerable to climate shocks.

Who We Are

Building Climate-Resilient Farming Communities

Sun & Soil Foundation is a Malawian non-profit organisation dedicated to improving rural livelihoods through solar-powered irrigation, climate-smart agriculture, renewable energy solutions, and inclusive economic development.

We work alongside farmers, women, youth, and vulnerable communities to create sustainable pathways out of poverty.

Featured Project

Nkhata Bay District
Solar Irrigation

Solar Irrigation Expansion in Nkhata Bay

In partnership with local farmer groups in Nkhata Bay, we deployed a high-capacity solar-powered drip irrigation system serving over 50 households. The project replaced costly and unreliable diesel pumps with clean, maintenance-free solar technology.
