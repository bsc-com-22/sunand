The Challenge We Address

Malawi's food system is under pressure. We are working to solve the critical issues that keep millions in a cycle of food insecurity and poverty.

Microeconomic pressures include rising input prices, high fuel costs, limited credit, market informality, and climate shocks like droughts and erratic rainfall.
