How Escrow Works

Eliminating Fraud Permanently

Escrow is the foundation of Sogoloh's security.

When a buyer pays, funds are held in a neutral account—not with the seller. The seller can see payment is confirmed, but cannot withdraw it until the buyer approves delivery.

This protects buyers from fake sellers who disappear with money. It protects sellers from buyers who claim non-delivery after receiving goods. No one can cheat the system.
