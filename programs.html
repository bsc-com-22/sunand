What We Do

Four programs, one integrated approach to resilient food systems.

Each programme works hand-in-hand with the others - clean energy powers the irrigation, irrigation enables climate-smart agriculture, training transforms farmers into entrepreneurs, and inclusive enterprise development ensures women and youth lead the change.

Solar-Powered Irrigation

Reliable solar energy systems that enable year-round production while reducing dependence on rainfall and fuel-powered irrigation.

  • Year-round crop production independent of rainfall
  • Elimination of diesel costs and emissions
  • 180W–1000W solar pump systems deployed in farming communities
  • Improved water security for smallholder farmers

Climate-Smart Agriculture

Sustainable farming techniques that improve productivity, soil health, and resilience to climate change.

  • Conservation agriculture and soil regeneration
  • Crop diversification for nutrition and income
  • Efficient water use and drip irrigation practices
  • Adaptation to changing rainfall and temperature patterns

Capacity Building & Mindset Change

Practical training programmes that equip farmers, youth, and women with technical and entrepreneurial skills.

  • Technical training on irrigation and agronomy
  • Business and financial literacy
  • Market linkage and value-chain coaching
  • Mindset shift from subsistence to market orientation

Women & Youth Enterprise Development

Inclusive economic growth through entrepreneurship, leadership development, and livelihood opportunities.

  • Women-led agribusiness incubation
  • Youth agripreneurship training
  • Leadership and mentorship pathways
  • Access to tools, inputs, and markets
