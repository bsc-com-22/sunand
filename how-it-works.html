How Sogoloh Protects Your Transactions

Our secure escrow system ensures your payment is held safely until delivery is confirmed, providing comprehensive buyer and seller protection every step of the way.

The Complete Process

5 Steps to Safe Trading

From signup to payment release—here's exactly how it works

01

Sign Up & Verify

Create your account in minutes. Upload your National ID for KYC verification. Verified users get a trust badge and access to secure transactions.

02

Create or Find Listing

Sellers post items with photos, descriptions, and prices. Buyers browse verified listings, contact sellers, and negotiate terms—all within the platform.

03

Buyer Pays into Escrow

Buyer deposits funds via Airtel Money, TNM Mpamba, or bank transfer. Sogoloh holds the payment securely—seller sees funds are committed, but cannot access them until delivery is approved.

04

Delivery & Inspection

Seller ships or delivers the item. Buyer inspects it carefully to confirm it matches the listing. If there's an issue, buyer can reject and request a refund or open a dispute.

05

Release Payment to Seller

Once the buyer approves, funds are instantly released from escrow to the seller's wallet. Seller can withdraw to mobile money or bank. Transaction complete—both parties protected.
