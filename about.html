About Sun & Soil

A Malawian foundation transforming food systems through clean energy.

Sun & Soil Foundation is a Malawian non-profit organization dedicated to transforming food systems through clean energy, climate-smart agriculture, and capacity development.

Our Vision

A Malawi where every community is food secure, climate-resilient, economically empowered, and powered by clean energy.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable households through solar-powered agriculture, climate-smart farming, and inclusive livelihood programmes.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do.

Sustainability

Long-term environmental and economic resilience in every intervention.

Inclusion

Women, youth, and vulnerable groups at the centre of our work.

Innovation

Renewable energy and modern agronomy adapted for rural Malawi.

Accountability

Transparent operations and measurable, reported outcomes.

Empowerment

Skills, tools, and confidence so communities lead their own change.

Environmental Stewardship

Healthy soils, efficient water use, and a thriving ecosystem.

Organisational Background

Renewable energy, practical training, enterprise development.

We combine the deployment of renewable energy infrastructure with hands-on training and enterprise development to strengthen Malawi's food systems. By placing solar-powered irrigation at the heart of climate-smart agriculture and pairing it with structured skills programmes for farmers, women and youth, we help rural communities shift from rain-fed subsistence farming to climate-resilient, market-oriented livelihoods.
