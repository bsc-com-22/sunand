About Sun & Soil Foundation

Powering Malawi's Food Future Through Solar Agriculture and Climate-Resilient Livelihoods

Who Are We?

Building Climate-Resilient Food Systems Through Clean Energy and Skills

Sun & Soil Foundation is a Malawian non-profit organisation strengthening rural livelihoods by integrating solar powered irrigation, climate-smart agriculture, and structured capacity building. We support smallholder farmers, women, youths, and all vulnerable groups at risk to move from climate-vulnerable subsistence farming toward productive, market-oriented, and resilient agricultural systems.

Our work combines renewable energy deployment, hands-on training, and inclusive enterprise development, aligned with Malawi Vision 2063, the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), and ESG principles.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable households through solar-powered agriculture, climate-smart farming, and inclusive livelihood programmes.

Our Vision

A Malawi where every community is food secure, climate-resilient, economically empowered, and powered by clean energy.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

Inclusion

Innovation

Accountability

Key Challenges

  • Rising input prices (fertilizer, pesticides, seeds)
  • High fuel and transport costs limiting mechanization
  • Limited access to credit and affordable financing
  • Market informality and weak negotiation power
  • Climate shocks: droughts, dry spells, erratic rainfall
